Why is a raven like a writing desk?

My Aunt has to be one of the sweetest women I know. She is strong yet soft-spoken, determined yet easy-going. When you're a guest in her home, she treats you to a wonderfully warm welcome.

As an aunt, she is a delightful, and as a wife and mother she is even more incredible. My cousins really lucked out when they were blessed with her as a mom, as their ability to pursue their passions showcase how much time and energy their parents spend making that possible.

Her husband Mark can attest that they make an incredible team who not only provide for their children but also work together to accomplish goals on a daily basis

If you're lucky enought to spend time with this woman the you will know all of this to be true. She is an incredible role model for anyone that learns by example of how to be and thoughful, loving person and we are lucky to have her in our family.
