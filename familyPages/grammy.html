She's in like Flynn again!

Carole Militello is one of the most life-loving, tenacious women out there. There is rarely a moment she isn't smiling about something, or making those around her smile just the same no matter the weather or worry. If you're lucky enough to have a grandmother half as wonderful as mine, you might as well have won the lottery.

In addition to providing much needed joy just by being in the room, she is also a tenacious card shark, so if you're looking for a compatriot to crush the competition in euchre, look no further. While her talent brings many-a-game to a close, she will also make sure you enjoy the entire experience. All's fair in love and war, but you won't know the difference because you're just having too much fun.

Her husband of 62 years, Frank, can attest to her next set of accolades, i.e. that of loving wife and mother. Her entire family will tell you, myself included, that a hug from this woman is enough to warm your heart for days. While you may not have the pleasure of being a member of her family, friends of hers can experience one in the same... on occasion.

Without the help and guidance of my wonderful grandmother, I would not be the man I am today. Her kindness is impossibly contagious and helps me carry forward treating others how I want to be treated myself. I feel very sorry for everyone that does not get the pleasure of knowing her, as I KNOW that anyone who does is all the better for it.
