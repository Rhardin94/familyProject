It's the Man! The Patriarch! You ARE the Father!

Jeff Hardin, accurately described as THE founding father in the case of our family, has always been someone that strives to stand up for what is right. When not carrying out his own philosophy, he is literally upholding the laws of the land and sharing those principles with his constituents... and I don't just mean his relatives.

When not dishing out legal counsel, my father is honoring his duty as a loyal and loving husband of soon to be 30 years to his wife Nancy. He has always been one half of the foundation that has kept us going through tough times, as well as enjoying the bounty of their joint hard work during the stable moments too.

Many of those good times involved enjoying, as I said, the fruits of their labor (i.e. grillin up some Lawry's seasoned burgers) and spending time together around the table as we reminisced about what movies we've watched recently or funny photos that we just had to share.

My dad has always been one of my staunchest supporters, always been one to laugh at stupid jokes with, to enjoy some awesome fried chicken while i'm hiding an awful hairdo underneath a beanie with, and to talk to about things that I don't always want to talk about initially. He supports without judgement, offers his opinion and advice when it's needed, and made sure we always know we can count on him to be there when we need him the most. I am so grateful to have a wonderful role model in my life and I don't know what I would do without him.
